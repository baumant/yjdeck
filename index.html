Grab Ur Mans

A TIGHT PRODUCTION

Concept

an interactive side scrolling music video that follows yung jake through a club being pestered by an annoying drunk boyfriend. using rag doll physics and other 2d physics simulation, the website allows you to interact with items on the page (bottles on tables, liquid in drinks, barf, cocktail waitresses, ones floating and, most importantly the annoying drunk bf)

Style

the video is reminiscent of a side scroller video game that cuts to different compositions. sometimes we'll be wide seeing the entire room. sometimes we'll be close up to see details.

Interaction

YJ will be the only constant throughout the video. he raps and moves forward through the scenes on his own. however virtually every other object in every scene, including drunk bf, is interactive to the user. the user sort of plays god in that they can move every object around via click and drag.

Item examples

  • glasses and liquid
  • drunk bf
  • barf
  • pee
